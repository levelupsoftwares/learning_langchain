Why These Specs Matter for Your Workflow
RAM (16GB Minimum): Agentic frameworks (like CrewAI, AutoGen, or LangGraph) often require running multiple processes, a local database (like Pinecone/ChromaDB), and an IDE simultaneously. 16GB is the baseline; avoid 8GB models unless you plan to upgrade immediately.

Processor (8th Gen+): Do not go below Intel 8th Gen or Ryzen 3000 series. Agentic engineering relies heavily on asynchronous programming (asyncio in Python). Newer processors handle these concurrent tasks much more efficiently.

Storage (NVMe SSD): You will be handling large environment files and potentially model weights. An NVMe SSD is non-negotiable for fast read/write speeds during development.
